Hi, I'm 6 weeks pregnant. What should I expect at my first prenatal visit?

10:02 AM

Congratulations on your pregnancy! At your first prenatal visit, typically around 8 weeks, you can expect:

  • A full medical history review
  • Physical exam and weight check
  • Blood tests to check for blood type, Rh factor, and screening for certain conditions
  • Urine test
  • Calculation of your due date
  • Discussion about prenatal vitamins and nutrition

Your healthcare provider will also answer any questions you have. It's a good idea to write down any concerns beforehand.
